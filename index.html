About My Mission

For over seven years, my work has focused on tracking environmental anomalies across localized forest ecosystems. I believe that integrating data analytics with fieldwork offers the cleanest path to impactful preservation.

Key areas of active engagement include:

  • Soil microbiota sequencing and health profiling.
  • Strategic planning for community-driven green belts.
  • Reforestation algorithms that mimic natural density scattering.
